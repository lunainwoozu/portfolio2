FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

ACTON II BLUETOOTH

IS THIS SPEAKER PORTABLE?

  • No, the Acton II Bluetooth isn't portable, it doesn't have a battery. But we do offer portable speakers here.

IS THERE AN APP FOR THIS SPEAKER?

  • Yes, this speaker can be used with the Marshall Bluetooth app.

WHAT IS THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN ACTON BLUETOOTH AND ACTON II BLUETOOTH?

  • The Acton II Bluetooth has had some upgrades compared to the Acton Bluetooth:

    • The Acton II Bluetooth has a different design (aesthetics) and you can use the Marshall Bluetooth app to control the speaker
    • The Acton II Bluetooth uses Bluetooth v5.0 while the Acton Bluetooth uses Bluetooth v4.0

    Please note that the Marshall Bluetooth App will only connect with the Acton II Bluetooth, Stanmore II Bluetooth and Woburn II Bluetooth speakers. It does not support any other Marshall Speakers.

DOES THIS PRODUCT HAVE aptX OR AAC FEATURE?

  • No, this product doesn't have the aptX or AAC feature.
